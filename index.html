Michael (MJ) Lyle

Michael Lyle (MJ to some) is an award-winning journalist for the Nevada Current . Michael graduated UNLV with an Bachelor of Arts in journalism and media study and has worked as a journalist since 2010. Michael is a graduate student at Syracuse University studying journalism innovation.

Prior to Nevada Current, he worked for the Las Vegas Review-Journal. His byline has been featured in The Washington Post, The Sunday and Desert Companion.

In his spare time, Michael is an amateur home cook, avid running, news junky and lover of horror movies He is based in Southern Nevada.
